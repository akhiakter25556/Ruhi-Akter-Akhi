## ğŸ¨ Customization

To customize this portfolio for your own use:

1. **Personal Information**: Update the content in all components with your information
2. **Projects**: Replace the projects array in `src/components/Projects.jsx` with your own projects
3. **Skills**: Modify the skills in `src/components/Skills.jsx` to match your expertise
4. **Images**: Replace images in the `src/assets` folder with your own
5. **Colors**: Customize the color scheme in `src/index.css`
6. **Resume**: Add your resume PDF to the `public` folder as `resume.pdf`
